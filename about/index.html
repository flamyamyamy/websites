Who I Am

My name is Giulia Amy Lyra Yuna Thiex, a 19-year-old developer from Villach, Austria, with roots in the United States. I have been programming for over four years and specialize in building advanced Discord bots, scalable backend systems, and analytical tooling.


Technical Focus

My strongest languages are Java and JavaScript, which I use to create performant, maintainable, and scalable applications. My primary focus lies in Discord bot development, backend logic, automation systems, and stock market analytics tooling.


Community Management

Beyond development, I actively work in moderation and staff management, helping structure and maintain large online communities through efficient workflows, scalable moderation systems, and well-organized team hierarchies.


My Approach

What sets me apart is my ability to combine technical development with structured organizational thinking. My background in legal studies and systems-oriented problem solving influences how I design software: with a strong emphasis on reliability, logical architecture, maintainability, and long-term scalability.


Beyond Tech

Outside of programming, I enjoy cooking, working out at the gym, riding motorcycles, and traveling — activities that help me stay disciplined, focused, and continuously open to new perspectives.
